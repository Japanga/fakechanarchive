FakeChan Archive is a manually operated live archive of threads on the FakeChan site that were visited and used by guests. Only "guest parties" are archived, and ones with high amounts of users. Your uploads to FakeChan will likely remain there for a long while, but eventually have to be removed if we get too close to the storage limits. Moderation on FakeChan is very lax, but I will instantly delete any unlikable images from the feed if they are uploaded.
